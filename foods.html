Food & Beverage

End-to-end SAP expertise for food producers, processors and FMCG companies - from farm to shelf, with full traceability and food safety compliance.

Food industry precision,
from recipe to shelf.
The food and beverage industry operates under some of the most demanding requirements in manufacturing - stringent food safety regulations, complex recipe and formulation management, tight shelf life windows and end-to-end traceability obligations. We bring deep SAP expertise specifically shaped for food producers, processors and FMCG companies operating in regulated markets.

Our consultants have hands-on experience implementing SAP across the full food value chain - from agricultural procurement and raw material intake, through production and quality control, to cold chain logistics and retail distribution. We understand the specific challenges of catch weight handling, allergen management, seasonal demand planning and multi-jurisdiction labelling compliance.

Whether you produce packaged foods, beverages, dairy, meat and seafood, confectionery or ready meals, we design SAP solutions that fit your operational reality and regulatory obligations.

Food & Beverage solutions
Specialized SAP capabilities
01
Recipe & Formula Management
SAP PP and PP-PI master recipes, bills of material, production versions and formulation management for food and beverage production - including yield, co-product and by-product handling.
02
Batch Traceability & Recall
Complete upstream and downstream batch traceability from raw material suppliers to finished goods. Rapid recall execution, batch where-used reporting and regulatory notification support in SAP.
03
Shelf Life Management
SAP shelf life management with best-before and use-by date tracking, FEFO (First Expired, First Out) inventory strategies, minimum remaining shelf life enforcement and customer-specific shelf life requirements.
04
Food Safety & Quality
SAP QM for HACCP-aligned inspection plans, allergen management, certificate of analysis generation, supplier quality monitoring and customer specification management. IFS, BRC and FSSC 22000 readiness.
05
Catch Weight & Variable Quantity
SAP catch weight processing for products sold by nominal weight but managed by actual weight - critical for fresh meat, fish, deli and bulk produce. Dual unit of measure management across the entire supply chain.
06
Cold Chain & Distribution
SAP EWM for temperature-controlled warehouse management, SAP TM for cold chain transport execution, and IBP for demand-driven supply chain planning - with full seasonal and promotional uplift modelling.
