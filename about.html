About Zurich Digital Solutions

A boutique Swiss consulting firm built on deep SAP expertise, regulated industry knowledge and uncompromising quality.

Who we are.
Zurich Digital Solutions is a specialist SAP and digital consulting firm headquartered in Zurich, Switzerland. We serve pharmaceutical, manufacturing, chemicals and medical device companies across Europe and beyond — delivering SAP implementations, integrations, data governance and digital transformation with Swiss precision.

Founded on the belief that consulting engagements should be delivered with the same exacting standards as the industries we serve, Zurich DS has built a reputation for quality, reliability and deep domain expertise.

We are a boutique firm by design — every client engagement is led by senior consultants with direct accountability for outcomes. No junior-heavy teams, no knowledge transfer gaps.
